Rules Statement

As organisers we recognise the rules for a game facilitate a story, we however believe they do not make a story.

Unlike other VtM systems such as Minds Eye Theatre. Raves and Revolts will be a mechanics lite game focusing on precalibration and calibration. As such we are not currently focusing on rules.

When rules are released they will be guidlines on how certain clans may be effected by their weakness' and suggestions on how their discplines can facilitate interesting scenes and moments.

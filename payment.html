Payment Guidelines

To participate in our Vampire: The Masquerade LARP, please follow the payment guidelines below:

    Step 1: Visit the PayPal website Step 2: Log in to your PayPal account (or create a new one) Step 3: Click on the "Send & Request" tab Step 4: Enter the payment amount and our PayPal email address Step 5: Add a note with your name and "R&R LARP" Step 6: Review the payment details and click "Send"

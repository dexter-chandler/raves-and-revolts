Our Pricing

The flat price for our game is £45

We will be accepting deposits of £15 in the coming weeks to help pay for venue deposit and to lock in our players.

Concessions

We recognise that everyone has different financial backgrounds, because of this we wish to offer £35 concession price for any of our players who feel they need it.

These reasons could be but limited to the following

  • Low Income
  • A parent/s
  • A student
  • Simply in hard times
  • Any other reason

    • We expect no proof. As organisers who have, and currently do, fall within this categories recognise that "proving" these can be demeaning and frustrating. If you need any further help please do not hesitate to contact us.

    Payment Guidelines

    To participate in our Vampire: The Masquerade LARP, please follow the payment guidelines below:

      Step 1: Visit the PayPal website

      Step 2: Log in to your PayPal account (or create a new one)

      Step 3: Click on the "Send & Request" tab

      Step 4: Enter the payment amount and our PayPal email address

      Step 5: Add a note with your name and "R&R LARP"

      Step 6: Review the payment details and click "Send"
